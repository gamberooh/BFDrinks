Where are we?

The Higher Education Institute 'Belluzzi-Fioravanti' was established on September 1st, 2011
following the merger of two historic Bolognese institutions, the 'Odone Belluzzi' Technical Industrial Institute and the 'Aristotele Fioravanti'
Professional Institute for Industry and Crafts.
The creation of the new school aimed to establish a technical-professional hub in the southwest area of Bologna,
capable of adequately interpreting the objectives set out by the reorganization of secondary education
and the birth of the new regional system of education and vocational training. The single headquarters in Via Cassini
now accommodates all students of the 'Belluzzi-Fioravanti' I.H.S.; it is located on the border between the Municipality of Bologna,
to which it belongs, and the Municipality of Casalecchio di Reno."
